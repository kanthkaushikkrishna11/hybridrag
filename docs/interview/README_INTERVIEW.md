## üí° Pro Tips

### Technical Interview

1. **Start with problem understanding**: Always clarify requirements
2. **Think aloud**: Explain your thought process
3. **Consider edge cases**: Show comprehensive thinking
4. **Write clean code**: Even in live coding, maintain quality
5. **Ask questions**: Show curiosity and engagement

### Project Discussion

1. **Know your architecture**: Be ready to draw diagrams
2. **Explain trade-offs**: Why you chose specific approaches
3. **Discuss challenges**: What problems you solved
4. **Quantify results**: Use metrics (2-3x improvement, etc.)
5. **Show growth**: What you learned from the project

### Behavioral Interview

1. **Use STAR method**: Situation, Task, Action, Result
2. **Be specific**: Use concrete examples
3. **Show impact**: Quantify results where possible
4. **Be honest**: Don't oversell or undersell
5. **Ask good questions**: Show genuine interest
